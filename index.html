Recipe #1

  • Noodles made of wheat and eggs were known in the Mediterranean world in antiquity, but the pasta we know as spaghetti—which takes its name from the Italian spaghetto, “little cord”
Meal Ingredients Price
seafood spaghetti
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) box spaghetti noodles
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce 1 (16 ounce) box spaghetti noodles 1 - 1 1⁄2 lb hamburger meat
$14

Step 1: On medium heat melt the butter and sautee the onion and bell peppers.
Step 2:Add the hamburger meat and cook until meat is well done.
Step 3:Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Step 4:Salt, pepper and garlic powder can be adjusted to your own tastes.
Step 5:Cook noodles as directed.
Step 6:Mix the sauce and noodles if you like, I keep them separate.
